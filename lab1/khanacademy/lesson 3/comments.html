Recipe #1

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Tuna fillet (Saku-grade) 300 g
Sushi rice 150 g
Avocado 1 pc.
Soy sauce 3 tbsp. l.
Sesame oil 1 tbsp. l.

Step 1: Rice preparation. Rinse the rice and boil it according to the instructions on the package. It should remain slightly sticky. Leave it to cool to room temperature.

Step 2: Slicing fish: Cut fresh tuna into cubes with a side of about 1.5â€“2 cm. It is important to use only the highest quality fish (shushi-grade).

Step 3: Marinating: In a deep bowl, combine soy sauce, sesame oil, finely chopped green onion and a pinch of chili flakes. Add the tuna, mix gently and let it stand in the fridge for 5-10 minutes.

Step 4: Assembling the dish: Place the rice in deep bowls. Spread the marinated tuna and sliced avocado on top.

Step 5: The final touch: Sprinkle with sesame seeds and, if desired, add a little nori seaweed or grated ginger.
